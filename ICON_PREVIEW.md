# 🚛 Your New Robby Truck Icon

## This is what appears on your phone's home screen!

### Icon Design
- **Background**: Blue (#2563eb)
- **Truck**: White with gray details
- **Wheels**: Black with gray rims
- **Windshield**: Light blue
- **Style**: Clean, simple, easy to recognize

### How It Looks on Your Phone

When installed, you'll see this blue truck icon on your home screen with the name:
**"Robby - Truck Assistant"** or **"Robby"** (depending on space)

```
┌─────────────────┐
│                 │
│   🚛 ╭────╮     │    ← White truck
│     ╱      ╲    │
│    │  ○  ○ │   │    ← Wheels
│                 │
│  BLUE BACKGROUND│
│                 │
└─────────────────┘
    Robby
```

### What Happens When You Tap It

1. **Tap the truck icon** on your home screen
2. **Robby app opens** immediately
3. **You see the home screen** with all your trucking features:
   - 🛣️ Truck Stops
   - 🧭 Navigation
   - ⛽ Fuel Logs
   - 📊 Mileage Tracker
   - 📝 Notes
   - 🎤 Voice Commands

### Installation

Run the one-button launcher and the icon will be automatically installed:

```bash
# On Mac/Linux:
./open-app.sh

# On Windows:
open-app.bat
```

Choose your platform (1 for iOS, 2 for Android), and the app with the truck icon will be installed on your device!

**That's it! Just look for the blue truck on your home screen and tap it!** 🚛📱✨
