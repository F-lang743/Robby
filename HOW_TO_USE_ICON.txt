🎯 WHAT IT IS:

A blue square icon with a white truck design that shows on your 
phone's home screen. When you tap it, Robby opens!

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🚀 HOW TO GET IT ON YOUR PHONE:

1. Open your terminal/command prompt

2. Run ONE command:
   
   Mac/Linux:  ./open-app.sh
   Windows:    open-app.bat

3. Choose your platform:
   
   Type 1 and press Enter (for iOS)
   Type 2 and press Enter (for Android)

4. Wait 2-3 minutes for installation

5. Look at your phone's home screen

   You'll see: [Blue Square with White Truck]
               Robby

6. TAP THE TRUCK ICON → ROBBY OPENS! ✨
